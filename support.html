Common Questions

How do I play?

Slide numbered tiles into the empty space. Arrange the numbers in order from 1 to 15 to solve the puzzle.

Why is Daily Challenge 4×4 only?

Daily Challenge uses a fixed 4×4 board so all players compete on the same puzzle each day.

Does the app collect my data?

The app does not directly collect personal information. Ads are served by Google AdMob, and leaderboards use Apple Game Center.

How can I report a problem?

Please email us with your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.
