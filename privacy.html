Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

15 Card Puzzle does not sell user data. The app is supported by ads served through Google AdMob.

Overview

15 Card Puzzle is a classic sliding puzzle game. We designed the app to avoid collecting personal information directly from users.

Information We Collect

We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not directly collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, photos, or precise location through the app.

Advertising

The app displays ads served by Google AdMob. Google AdMob and its advertising partners may automatically collect or process certain information to provide, limit, measure, and improve ads, such as device identifiers, approximate location, ad interactions, diagnostics, and related technical information.

Any data processed by Google AdMob is handled according to Google's applicable advertising and privacy policies. Users may manage ad personalization choices through device settings and Google privacy controls where available.

Game Center

If you use leaderboards, Apple Game Center may process your Game Center profile and scores. This is handled by Apple under Apple’s privacy practices. The app only submits gameplay scores such as move count for leaderboard functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. We do not share personal information with data brokers. Third-party services used by the app, such as Google AdMob and Apple Game Center, may process data as described in their own policies.

Children’s Privacy

15 Card Puzzle is available in all countries and is intended as a general-audience puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us so we can address the issue.

International Availability

The app is available globally. Data processed by third-party services may be transferred or processed in countries other than where you live, according to those services’ policies and applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
